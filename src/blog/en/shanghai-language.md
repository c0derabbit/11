### Daily linguistics:  

香蕉 smell + banana = banana  
香料 smell + material = spice  
香草 smell + grass = vanilla  
香菜 smell + vegetable = coriander  
香水 smell + water = perfume  
香吻 smell + kiss = kiss  
香油 smell + oil = sesame oil  
香港 smell + bay = Hong Kong.
